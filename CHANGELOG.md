## [0.1.0] - 2026-06-25

### Added

- Fictional, structured traceability-register input for practitioner drafting and review workflows.
- Validation for stable control IDs, source framework/reference/version, ownership, review status, and evidence records.
- Machine-readable JSON Schema and fictional sample input.
- Public input-contract and migration documentation.
- Methodology, source-provenance, and public-release review materials.

### Scope

This is an early practitioner prototype for structuring traceability and review artifacts. It does not provide legal advice, compliance certification, conformity assessment, safety-case approval, regulatory approval, or production-release approval.
